Introduction

This project focuses on analyzing the electric vehicle (EV) population dataset to understand adoption trends, manufacturer market share, and clean fuel incentive eligibility. Using Power BI, I created a single-page landing dashboard with an interactive custom tooltip page to allow stakeholders to view high-level KPIs while drilling into detailed vehicle information on hover.

Objective

To provide a clear and interactive view of EV adoption from 2010 to 2024.

Key objectives include:

  • To identify the distribution of BEV and PHEV vehicles, top manufacturers, and most popular models./li>
  • To determine the percentage of vehicles eligible for Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle (CAFV) incentives.
  • To display geographical distribution of EVs at the state and county levels for targeted infrastructure planning.

    • Dataset Details

  • Source: Electric Vehicle Population Data (CSV format).
  • Records: 150,482 EV registrations.
  • Key Fields: VIN (1–10), Make, Model, Model Year, Electric Vehicle Type, Electric Range, CAFV Eligibility, State, County, City, Vehicle Location (WKT).
  • Period Covered: 2010 – 2024.

    • Data Cleaning & Transformation

    Performed in Power Query:

  • Removed duplicate entries based on VIN (1–10).
  • Standardized Make and Model text format.
  • Filtered out invalid or zero values in Electric Range.
  • Categorized Electric Vehicle Type into BEV and PHEV for KPIs.

    • Data Modeling

    The data model was designed using a star schema approach for simplicity and performance:

    Fact Table:

    EV Registrations – contains transactional records for each registered vehicle with details such as VIN (1–10), Make, Model, Model Year, Electric Vehicle Type, Electric Range, CAFV Eligibility, State, County, City, and coordinates.

    Dimension Tables:

  • Dim Make – unique list of vehicle manufacturers.
  • Dim Model – unique list of vehicle models linked to their makes.
  • Dim Location – County, City, State, and geographic coordinates extracted from WKT for mapping.
  • Dim CAFV Eligibility – eligibility status descriptions for filtering and KPI calculations.

    • Dashboard Design

    Electric Vehicle Analysis

  • KPIs: Total Vehicles, BEV Count, PHEV Count, % BEV, Avg Electric Range, CAFV Eligible Count.
  • Trend chart: EV registrations by model year.
  • Geographic map: Vehicle count by county.
  • Top manufacturers and top models lists.

    • Tooltip Page

    Delivered a complete exploratory data analysis (EDA) report with visualizations.

    Key Insights

  • BEV dominance: 77.6% of total registrations are BEVs.
  • Top manufacturer: Tesla (~46% market share).
  • Top models: Model Y, Model 3, and Nissan Leaf.
  • CAFV Eligibility: Over 53% of registered EVs qualify for CAFV incentives.
  • Geographical focus: Majority of registrations concentrated in Washington State.
  • Trend: Significant growth from 2018 onwards, peaking in 2023.

    • Conclusion

    Delivered an interactive, single-page dashboard enhanced with tooltip-driven details, enabling users to quickly assess EV adoption patterns while exploring specific model and eligibility details without leaving the main page.
