Introduction

This project involved analyzing pizza sales data from a restaurant's order management system using SQL. The aim was to evaluate sales performance, identify best-selling pizza types, analyze order patterns by size and time, and uncover revenue-generating insights to support operational and marketing decisions.

Objective

To perform structured SQL queries on the pizza sales database to uncover ordering patterns, top-performing products, category-wise performance, and revenue trends â€” helping the business make informed, data-driven decisions.

Methodology

Database Exploration:

  1. Reviewed the pizza_types, pizzas, orders, and order_details tables.
  2. Explored the menu_items and order_details tables to examine available data.

Order Volume & Revenue Analysis:

Retrieved the full list of menu items.

  • Total number of orders placed.
  • Least and most expensive items.
  • Highest-priced pizza available on the menu.

    • Order Data Analysis:

  • The most common pizza size ordered.
  • Top 5 most frequently ordered pizza types by quantity.
  • Top 3 most ordered pizza types based on total revenue.

    • Category-wise Sales Insights:

    Performed an INNER JOIN on tables to merge information with details.

    • Used JOIN operations to combine relevant tables.
    • Calculated total quantity sold for each pizza category.
    • Determined revenue contribution percentages for each pizza type.

    Time-based Analysis:

    • Analyzed distribution of orders by hour of the day.
    • Grouped orders by date to calculate the average number of pizzas ordered per day.
    • Analyzed cumulative revenue growth over time.

    Revenue Segmentation:

    • Identified top 3 pizza types by revenue within each category.
    • Provided a breakdown of category-wise revenue performance.

    Results

    • Calculated total order count and overall sales revenue.
    • Identified the top-selling pizza types and sizes.
    • Revealed that specific pizza sizes dominated customer preferences.
    • Pinpointed the most profitable pizza types and categories.
    • Uncovered order timing trends, showing peak ordering hours.
    • Delivered insights on cumulative revenue growth over time.
    • Provided revenue percentage contribution analysis for each pizza type.
    • Presented recommendations for menu optimization and promotional focus based on ordering and revenue trends.
